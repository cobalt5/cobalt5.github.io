Projects

Pumpkin Head AR Experience

An augmented reality project that places a pumpkinhead onto people's heads. The pumpkin changes color based on distance from each person. This program has been used at my high school during Halloween, providing a fun and interactive experience for students and teachers alike. It showcases the potential of AR technology in creating engaging and immersive experiences.
